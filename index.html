Welcome to Medical Heads

Medical Heads was established to address unmet and under-served needs of foreign Orthopedics extremities companies entering the Japan market. We are dedicated to market entry solutions from early stage business establishment to 4 - 5 years of sales activities.
Over the past 9 years' we have a track-record of support public and private foreign global companies establish subsidiaries and direct sales organizations. Medical Heads operates under the following principles: respect of client’s preferences, respect for Medical Doctor’s preferences and a strong belief that through our business model we can create new and improved solutions  to the challenges faced in market entry now and for the future.

Medical Doctors are our primary focus here at Medical Heads. We listen to your organization and the challenges and future targets and respect what needs to be better.

We also seek to understand Medical Doctors' hesitations and frustrations with certain procedures and focus on how we can make them easier. The name “ Medical Heads” was chosen by design – to never lose focus on our goal to advance the business opportunities for our clients and look in to the future together with the customers we support.
